Cooking the perfect steak

Cooking the perfect steak at home is easier than you think, all thats needed is the right prep and a little know-how.

What you need

  • A 2 inch thick ribeye, strip or chuck steak
  • Kosher Salt
  • Cast iron skillet

How its done

  1. First things first, you need to prep your steak. Before cooking, let the steak rest at room temperature for an hour. This will help the steak cook more evenly when it hits the pan.
  2. Salt the steak. At the beginning of the rest period, liberally salt the meat with kosher salt. As the steak sits, the salt will draw moisture from the meat and over an hour the moisture will be reabsorbed, drawing salt into the steak. This results in both a juicer steak and a better sear.
  3. Cook the steak. In a lightly oiled, searing hot cast-iron skillet cook the steak for two minutes on each side for rare, 4 for medium.
