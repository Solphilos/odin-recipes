Classic Beef Stew

This classic dish is the definition of comfort food.
Packed with hearty chunks of beef, carrots and potatoes this stew is a perfect meal for any day of the week

What you'll need:

  • 1 pound chuck steak, cut into chunks
  • 2 whole carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 pound red potatoes, quartered
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon thyme

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper the beef chunks
  2. In lightly oiled pot, sear beef until brown on all sides, remove and set aside.
  3. Add butter to pot and Saute onions until caramelized, about 10 minutes.
  4. Slowly stir in flour, making a rouix.
  5. Add in beef, broth, thyme and rest of salt and pepper.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 2.5 hours, adding carrots and potatoes and cook another 40 minutes.
