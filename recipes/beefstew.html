Beef Stew

This classic dish is the definition of comfort food. Packed with hearty chunks of beef, carrots and potatoes this stew is a perfect meal for any day of the week

What you'll need:

  • 1 pound chuck steak, cut into chunks
  • 2 whole carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 pound red potatoes, quartered
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
